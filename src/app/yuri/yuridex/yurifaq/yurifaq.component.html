Q: What the hell is DynastyScans?

A: A very old website dedicated exclusively to yuri and yuri-adjacent titles. The reading experience is more rudimentary compared to a modern site design like mangadex, their tag pool is massive and with a level of specificity you won't find anywhere else. Dynasty also hosts a metric ton of doujins and fanmade content (of japanese origin), which is great for community-driven franchises like Touhou.

Q: Cool, but why are some translations on Mangadex so much far ahead of Dynasty?

A: Usually, translators will upload new chapters to both sites. But Dynasty has a very tight, old fashioned and quite naive anti-snipe policy, under which they only let one group upload chapters for a series, and won't allow anyone else to upload new chapters unless the original group explicitly says they dropped the project. So when a translation group goes AWOL on a series (aka: most of the time) those end up abandoned forever on Dynasty. I think you also need to contact the admins for permissions to take over a series, idk it's just dumb. I'm sure it worked wonders in 2010 when this was a niche, but now it's nothing but problems.

Also, Dynasty will often (though not always) halt uploads on mangas that are licensed; most commonly they won't allow chapters that are already available officially to be uploaded, but they often do permit a translation that is still ahead of the official releases to continue.

I've linked the dynasty page for all series that have one as an extra resource, not a reading site. My personal recommendation: Use Dynasty for browsing so you can take advantage of their near infinite and ever expanding super specific tag pool, and use Mangadex for tracking and reading.

Q: What the fuck is a yaya?

A: Coined from the homonymous character in Strawberry Panic, a yaya is a girl who's close to the protagonist, usually a childhood friend, and harbors feelings for her but ulimately loses to an outsider. A good yaya will have a mental breakdown over this, though it's not mandatory in order to qualify as a yaya.

TL;DR: it's the yuri version of every single girl in Makeine.
